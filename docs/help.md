## Getting Started

### What is Vanta?
A modern trading platform with two modes:
- **Pro Mode** — full charts, orders, stop-loss/take-profit, position management
- **Quick Mode** — fast, simple: pick a direction, pick a duration, win or lose at expiry

### Demo accounts
Every new account starts in demo with $10,000 in virtual funds. Real prices, fake money. Switch to live whenever you're ready (after identity verification).

### Switching between Pro and Quick
Profile tab → Trading Mode. Switches instantly. Same account, different way of trading.

## Trading

### Opening a trade (Pro)
1. Pick a symbol from the chips above the chart
2. Set your volume (lot size — 0.01 minimum)
3. Optional: set Stop Loss and Take Profit
4. Tap **Buy** (you think price goes up) or **Sell** (you think price goes down)

### Opening a round (Quick)
1. Pick an asset from the chips
2. Pick a duration (60s, 5min, 15min)
3. Pick your stake ($5 to $100)
4. Tap **Up** or **Down**

If price moves your way at expiry, you win the displayed payout (e.g., 1.85× stake). If not, you lose the stake.

### Stop-loss and take-profit
- **Stop Loss** — price level where the trade auto-closes to limit your loss
- **Take Profit** — price level where the trade auto-closes to lock in gains

Use them especially if you can't watch the chart.

## AI Robots

### What they do
You describe a strategy in plain English. Vanta's AI translates it into rules and runs it.

Examples:
- "Buy Amazon at NYSE open every weekday"
- "Send me 3 stock picks every morning"
- "Buy EURUSD when it dips 0.5% in 5 minutes"
- "Alert me when Bitcoin moves 3%"

### Tip robots vs. Trade robots
- **Trade robots** — actually open positions
- **Tip robots** — just send you ideas, you decide

Choose the right type when building. You can always pause or stop a robot.

## Money

### Depositing
Portfolio tab → **Deposit**. Crypto, bank wire, and cards available depending on region.

### Withdrawing
1. Complete identity verification (Profile → Identity Verification)
2. Portfolio tab → **Withdraw**
3. Funds arrive in 1–3 business days

### Fees
- **Spread** — built into the prices you see (no separate commission)
- **Overnight financing** — small fee on positions held past midnight UTC
- **No deposit/withdrawal fees** — we cover them

## Identity Verification (KYC)

Required before your first withdrawal. Takes about 2 minutes.

Documents needed:
- Government-issued ID (front and back)
- Selfie holding the ID
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)

Documents are encrypted and used only for compliance.
